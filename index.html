About Me!

Business Intelligence Analyst with over 8 years Project Engineering Experience in the Canadian Oil and Gas Industry delivering pipeline and facility projects across Alberta and British.

I have a Certificate in Business Intelligence from SAIT Polytechnic, Calgary, Alberta. I also have a Masters Degree in Engineering from the University of Calgary, Alberta. 

I most recently participated in a 2020 Datathon organized by Untapped Energy in collaboration with SPE Calgary. I was part of a team called Data Conquerors, we won a ‘People’s Choice Award’.

In my spare time, I love to work with children to promote literacy in topics that might not necessarily be covered in schools like Coding, Finance or Engineering.
