About Me!

Ijeoma is a seasoned Business Intelligence Analyst with over eight (8) years of Project Engineering Experience in the Canadian Energy Sector. She has led various pipeline and facility projects across Western Canada with a focus on Alberta and British Columbia, working for energy sector giants such as Shell Canada and OEL Projects Ltd.

She holds a Certificate in Business Intelligence from SAIT Polytechnic, Calgary, Alberta, a Masters Degree in Engineering(MEng) from the University of Calgary, Alberta, and a Bachelor of Science (First Class Division) in Chemical Engineering from the University of New Brunswick, Fredericton, New Brunswick. 

She most recently participated in a 2020 Datathon organized by Untapped Energy in collaboration with SPE Calgary. She played a pivotal role as part of the Data Conquerors team that won the ‘People’s Choice Award’.

She is also passionate about developing children, and so she volunteers to promote literacy in STEM Education and Personal Finance.
